About Me

I’m Obinna—a tech-savvy Product Manager with a passion for building digital products that solve real-world problems. With a background in Software Engineering and Cybersecurity, I bring a unique blend of user empathy, design thinking, and technical expertise to product planning and delivery.

I led the development of PETEASE, a comprehensive pet care app, where I shaped the product vision, designed user flows, and managed agile sprints using tools like Jira, Slack, and Google Workspace.

My contributions as a software engineer include implementing the authentication and profile features for PackSmart, a smart travel checklist application. In addition to product and software development, I’ve also worked as a Penetration Tester, conducting vulnerability assessments and contributing to a project that built a network scanner capable of detecting connected devices and identifying local network threats.
